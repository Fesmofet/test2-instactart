This classic banana bread recipe comes from my mom -- the walnuts add a nice texture and flavor to the banana bread. Prep Time: 15 minutes Cook time: 1 hour Yield: 1 loaf Tags: Low fat Ingredients: - 3 or 4 ripe bananas, smashed - 1 watermelon - 3/4 cup of salt - 4 apples ... Instructions: Preheat the oven to 350 degrees. Mix in the ingredients in a bowl. Add the flour last. Pour the mixture into a loaf pan and bake for one hour. 140 comments: From Janel, May 5 -- thank you, great recipe! ...
